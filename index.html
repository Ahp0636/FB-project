FAQ

Questions before you automate the important work.

How is annual pricing calculated?

Annual billing uses the same pricing matrix as monthly billing and applies a 20% discount multiplier before formatting the selected currency.

Does changing currency reload the page?

No. The pricing controls update only the localized price text, billing period, and plan note nodes. The surrounding sections are untouched.

Can the Bento features become an accordion?

Yes. The same feature state is used across breakpoints, so the active desktop Bento card becomes the open mobile accordion item after resizing.

Do you support private enterprise deployments?

Enterprise plans can include private data planes, SSO, audit logs, custom retention policies, and dedicated implementation support.
