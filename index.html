Yes. Teams can start a trial to explore workflow creation, tool connections, dashboards, and AI decisioning before choosing a paid plan.

AXIOM is built for enterprise control with audit logs, review states, role-aware workflows, and deployment options for sensitive operational data.

Can I integrate with my existing tools?

Yes. AXIOM connects with existing business tools through triggers, webhooks, APIs, documents, databases, inboxes, and human approval steps.

Enterprise plans support private deployments, SSO, custom workflow volume, audit requirements, and dedicated solution architecture.
